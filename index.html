Biography

I am a PhD candidate of Institute of Physics, Chinese Academy of Sciences, majoring in theory of condensed matter physics.

My specific research direction is quantum Monte Carlo simulations of strongly correlated electron models in condensed matter physics such as Hubbard model and Holstein model.

Interests

  • Strongly Correlated Electronic Systems
  • Developments of Numerical Methods
  • Quantum Spin liquids
